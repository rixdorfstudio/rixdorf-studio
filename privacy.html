2. What data we collect

We collect no personal data. The app stores the following information exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults):

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, play time)

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party. It never leaves your device.

3. In-App Purchases

Optional purchases are processed entirely by Apple Inc. via the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or process any payment or billing information. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy

4. App Store Analytics

Apple may collect aggregated, anonymised analytics about app downloads and usage through the App Store. This data is provided to us only in aggregate form (e.g. total downloads per country) and cannot be used to identify individual users. We have no control over this data collection by Apple.

5. No tracking, no advertising

This app contains no advertising, no tracking SDKs, no analytics frameworks, and does not request the App Tracking Transparency permission. No data is shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

Since we do not collect or process any personal data, these rights primarily apply to any communication you send us directly (e.g. support emails). To exercise any of these rights, contact: rixdorf.studio@gmail.com

7. Data retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. We retain no data on our servers because we operate no servers.

8. Children

This app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.
